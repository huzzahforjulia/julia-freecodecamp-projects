Please Please Me

1963: Their first album. "Shake it up baby now!"

Notable songs:

  • Twist And Shout
  • I Saw Her Standing There
  • Please Please Me
  • P.S. I Love You

With the Beatles

1963: Their second album. "Though you treat me badly, I love you madly"

Notable songs:

  • It Won't Be Long
  • Please Mister Postman
  • Roll Over Beethoven
  • You Really Got A Hold On Me
  • All My Loving

A Hard Day's Night

1964: This was the one about the night that belonged to the hard day. "It's been a hard day's night, and I've been working like a dog!"

Notable songs:

  • A Hard Day's Night
  • Can't Buy Me Love
  • Any Time At All
  • Tell Me Why

Beatles for Sale

1964: This is the one that no one really talks about. "I ain't got nothin but love, babe, eight days a week!"

Notable songs:

  • Eight Days A Week

Help!

1965: This one has some bangers. "Yesterday... All my troubles seemed so far away"

Notable songs:

  • Yesterday
  • Help!
  • You've Got To Hide Your Love Away
  • You're Going To Lose That Girl
  • Ticket To Ride

Rubber Soul

1965: Suddenly... Country ? "Of all these friends and lovers, there is no one compares with you"

Notable songs:

  • Drive My Car
  • Norweigan Wood There
  • In My Life

Revolver

1966: Now we're really getting somewhere! "All the lonely people, where do they all come from?"

Notable songs:

  • Eleanor Rigby
  • Yellow Submarine There
  • And Your Bird Can Sing
  • For No One

Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band

1967: Often stated to be the best album of all time. Diverse, interesting, expressive, masterful. "I read the news today, oh boy"

Notable songs:

  • With A Little Help From My Friends
  • Lucy In The Sky With Diamonds
  • Getting Better
  • She's Leaving Home
  • Being For The Benefit Of Mr. Kite!
  • When I'm Sixty Four
  • A Day In The Life

Magical Mystery Tour

1967: Underrated!! "Goo goo g'joob"

Notable songs:

  • Strawberry Fields Forever
  • All You Need Is Love
  • I Am The Walrus
  • Penny Lane
  • Your Mother Should Know
  • Hello, Goodbye
  • The Fool On The Hill

The White Album

1968: The long one. "Take these broken wings and learn to fly"

Notable songs:

  • Blackbird
  • While My Guitar Gently Weeps
  • Ob-La-Di, Ob-La-Da
  • Back In The U.S.S.R.
  • Julia
  • Honey Pie

Abbey Road

1969: My favourite one and probably the most popular & iconic one. "Little darling, the smiles returning to the faces"

Notable songs:

  • Here Comes The Sun
  • Come Together
  • Something
  • Oh! Darling
  • Octopus's Garden
  • You Never Give Me Your Money

Let it Be

1970: The last one. "Pools of sorrow, waves of joy, are drifting through my opened mind"

Notable songs:

  • Let It Be
  • Across The Universe
  • Get Back
  • Two Of Us
  • The Long And Winding Road
